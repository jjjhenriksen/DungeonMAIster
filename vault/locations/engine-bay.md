# Engine Bay

The engine bay feels like standing inside a metal lung that has started coughing fire.

## Sensory details

- Radiant heat baked into the floor grates
- Coolant mist catching the emergency strobes
- A whining harmonic buried under the reactor thrum

## Hidden hooks

- Hairline manifold fracture behind a maintenance plate
- Improvised brace that will not survive another major surge
