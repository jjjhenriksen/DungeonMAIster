# Shroud Reef

The Shroud Reef is a cathedral of translucent biomass, splintered hulls, and reflected starlight that never quite behaves like light should.

## Sensory details

- Rainbow sheens sliding across organic arches
- Wreckage fused into rib-like patterns
- Radio echoes arriving before the original signal

## Hidden hooks

- Sections of the Reef mirror human anatomical geometry
- Probe Khepri-9 may have been absorbed rather than destroyed
