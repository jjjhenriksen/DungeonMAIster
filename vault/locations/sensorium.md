# Sensorium

The sensorium is a listening chamber disguised as a lab, every surface tuned toward the black water of space outside the hull.

## Sensory details

- Soft sonar chimes from passive arrays
- Projected waveforms curling around the observation pit
- A chill from overclocked analysis racks

## Hidden hooks

- Echo Bloom pulse-trains align with human speech cadences
- A dead zone in the Reef that bends every mapping pass around it
