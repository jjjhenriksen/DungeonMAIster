# Med Bay

The med bay is too clean to be comforting right now. The sharper it smells, the more it implies someone is scrubbing away evidence.

## Sensory details

- Sterile gel and ozone in the air
- Biobed readouts casting ghost-pale light
- Locked cabinets clicking under remote authorization

## Hidden hooks

- Scan set showing matching filament growth in multiple crew members
- Recycled air samples with stress-reactive spores
