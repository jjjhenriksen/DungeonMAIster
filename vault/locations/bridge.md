# Bridge

The bridge is low-lit, quiet, and tense, with every console carrying a different failure nobody wants to say aloud first.

## Sensory details

- Blue tactical light over sleepless faces
- A constant sub-bass hull hum
- Navigation projections stuttering half a beat late

## Hidden hooks

- Sealed command archive with altered relay transcripts
- Orlov's flight path annotations tracing impossible loops through open space
