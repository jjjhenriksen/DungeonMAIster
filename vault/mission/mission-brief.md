# Operation Silent Aster

## Public brief

The RSV Ossa has been tasked with entering the Shroud Reef, locating the missing relay probe Khepri-9, and charting a stable route back out before the anomaly closes again.

## Strategic pressure

- The ship is already strained before insertion.
- Crew morale is brittle after a series of unexplained sensor incidents.
- Corporate command expects scientific gain, but the crew expects survival.

## DM secrets

- Khepri-9 transmitted a voiceprint from a dead crew member.
- A second objective exists: recover viable anomalous biomass if practical.
