# Anomaly Catalog

## Echo Bloom

A luminous floral knot of energy and spores that repeats speech before it is spoken. Exposure causes déjà vu, headaches, and compulsive attempts to "correct" conversations.

## Spore Front

A drifting veil of particulate life that migrates toward distress and elevated cortisol. Once inhaled, it appears to map the host's emotional state through subtle filament growth.

## Mirrored Canyons

A region of the Reef where scans loop, starfields invert, and short routes become long ones. Pilots describe the sensation as being watched by the geometry itself.
