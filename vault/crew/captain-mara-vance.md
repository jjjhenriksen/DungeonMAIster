# Captain Mara Vance

Commanding officer of the RSV Ossa. Vance is steady under pressure, but the missing relay probe has personal weight: one of the recovered voiceprints belongs to a former mentor she signed off as deceased.

## Public read

- Keeps the crew moving when fear would rather freeze them.
- Believes clear orders save lives, but hates what secrecy does to trust.

## Hidden prompt hooks

- Corporate black-file objective sits in Vance's sealed command packet.
- Rook suspects Vance is already withholding part of the probe transcript.
