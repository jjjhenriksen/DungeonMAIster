# Executive Officer Selene Rook

Rook presents as immaculate control under pressure. She is the kind of officer who can make a whispered correction feel like a verdict.

## DM hooks

- Carries a standing corporate recovery directive she has not disclosed to the full crew.
- Distrusts Dr. Vale's curiosity and thinks the mission is one bad decision from mutiny.
