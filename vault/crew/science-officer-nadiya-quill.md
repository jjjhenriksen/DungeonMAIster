# Science Officer Nadiya Quill

Quill is brilliant, obsessive, and a little too willing to let the unknown get close if it means understanding it. The Reef fascinates them in a way that borders on devotion.

## Public read

- Sees patterns where others see noise.
- Has the trust of Dr. Vale, which may be useful or dangerous.

## Hidden prompt hooks

- Quill has already heard the Echo Bloom repeat one of their own future sentences.
- They suspect the Reef is capable of selective imitation.
